We identified two major customer groups

Young consumers (18-30 years)

Meet 1-2 times a month - Usually consume finger food ("para picar")

Family consumers (30-45+ years)

Small gatherings 1-2 times per week - Order chefs for birthdays/social gatherings - Selection is made by word of mouth

Customers willingness to pay for an experience at home: $35

Main perceived benefit is ability to compare offers

Other benefits are no need to cook, focus on guests, sophisticated cuisine, simple

Customers would like to purchase additional services

Services mentioned included decoration, music and drinks.
